Nigeria

Nigeria is a diverse and vibrant country located in West Africa. Known for its rich cultural heritage, Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa, with over 200 million people. The country is home to more than 250 ethnic groups, each with its own unique traditions and languages. Nigeria boasts a strong economy driven by its vast natural resources, including oil and gas, as well as a growing tech and entertainment industry, particularly in Nollywood, the third-largest film industry in the world. Despite its challenges, Nigeria remains a dynamic and influential force on the African continent
